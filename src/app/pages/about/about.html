Our Mission

We provide compassionate, evidence-based mental health services combined with spiritual guidance to help individuals navigate trauma, particularly PTSD, depression, anxiety, and life transitions. Our approach honors Jewish traditions while embracing modern therapeutic practices.

Understanding PTSD

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD) is a mental health condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event. Symptoms may include:

  • Intrusive memories, flashbacks, or nightmares
  • Avoidance of reminders of the trauma
  • Negative changes in thinking and mood
  • Heightened reactions and difficulty sleeping

PTSD is treatable. With proper support, therapy, and sometimes medication, individuals can reclaim their lives and find healing.

Trauma in the Jewish Community

The Jewish community faces unique trauma experiences including:

  • Historical and intergenerational trauma
  • Antisemitism and hate-related incidents
  • Community violence and terror attacks
  • Immigration and displacement experiences

We recognize these distinct challenges and provide culturally competent care that honors your heritage while promoting healing.
